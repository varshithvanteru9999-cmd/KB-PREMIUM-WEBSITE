Last updated: 31 March 2026

We understand that plans change. Our policy is designed to be fair to both you and our team — who reserve time specifically for your appointment.

1 Cancellation Policy

We request adequate notice if you need to cancel, so we can offer your slot to another guest.

Notice Given Cancellation Fee Advance Refund
48+ hours before appointment None 100% refund
24–48 hours before appointment 25% of advance 75% refund
Less than 24 hours before appointment 50% of advance 50% refund
Same-day cancellation / No-show Full advance forfeited No refund

Cancellations must be communicated via phone or WhatsApp to +91 96404 01112. Online cancellations through the dashboard will also be honoured.

2 Rescheduling

We're happy to reschedule your appointment subject to availability:

  • Rescheduling is allowed up to 2 times per booking without any charge, provided it is done at least 24 hours before the original appointment time
  • Rescheduling within 24 hours is treated as a late cancellation and may attract charges as per the table above
  • Your advance payment will be carried forward to the new appointment date
  • To reschedule, contact us via WhatsApp or call +91 96404 01112

3 Refund Eligibility

Refunds are applicable only for advance payments made online through our payment gateway. The following conditions apply:

  • The cancellation request must be submitted within the eligible notice period (see table above)
  • Refunds are processed only to the original payment method (same bank account / UPI / card used for payment)
  • Cash payments collected in-salon are not eligible for online refunds — any adjustment will be made at your next visit
  • Partial services rendered at the salon are not eligible for refund
  • If a service caused dissatisfaction due to a genuine quality concern, please contact us within 48 hours of the appointment for a resolution

4 Refund Timeline

Once a refund is approved and initiated by us:

  • UPI & Net Banking: 2–5 business days
  • Credit / Debit Card: 5–7 business days
  • Wallets (PayU): 2–3 business days

Processing times depend on your bank and payment provider. We will send you a confirmation message once the refund has been initiated from our end. If you do not receive your refund within the stated period, please contact us with your booking reference number and transaction ID.

We aim to initiate all approved refunds within 2 business days of the cancellation confirmation.

5 Non-Refundable Situations

The following are not eligible for a refund under any circumstances:

  • Tattoo deposits — the design consultation fee and booking deposit for tattoo services are non-refundable as they cover the artist's design time and slot reservation
  • Completed services — once a service has been fully rendered, no refund will be issued regardless of the reason
  • No-shows — failure to arrive for a confirmed appointment without prior notice
  • Promotional / discounted bookings — services booked under special promotional pricing
  • Package deals — partially used packages or combo deals are not refundable for the unused portions
  • Gift vouchers — once redeemed, gift vouchers cannot be returned or refunded

Refunds will not be processed for dissatisfaction due to subjective preferences (e.g., "I don't like the colour") — we encourage all customers to discuss desired outcomes in detail with our stylist before the service begins.

6 Salon-Initiated Changes

In rare circumstances, we may need to cancel or reschedule your appointment due to:

  • Staff illness or emergency
  • Public holidays or unforeseen salon closure
  • Circumstances beyond our reasonable control

In such cases, we will notify you as soon as possible and offer either:

  • A full refund of any advance payment, or
  • A free rescheduled appointment at your preferred date and time

We sincerely apologise for any inconvenience caused in such situations.

7 How to Cancel or Request a Refund

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 96404 01112 or call us directly
  2. Provide your full name, booking reference number (e.g., Booking #21), and appointment date
  3. State whether you wish to cancel or reschedule
  4. We will confirm the cancellation and the applicable refund amount within 1 business day
  5. The refund will be initiated within 2 business days of confirmation

You may also cancel via the Customer Dashboard — the system will automatically calculate your refund eligibility based on the notice period.
